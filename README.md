# MLB-LAB

MLB-LAB is a baseball intelligence research repository.

The objective is not to build prediction models first.

The objective is to identify:

- Valuable MLB data sources
- Useful research workflows
- Predictive signals
- Decision support systems
- Automated reporting frameworks

---

## Repository Structure

### Blueprint

System architecture and roadmap.

### Research

Raw repository investigations and source reviews.

### Notes

Insights, analyses, comparisons, and principles.

### Reports

Finished intelligence reports and summaries.

### Prompts

Reusable analysis frameworks.

---

## Core Principle

Data is not the goal.

Intelligence is the goal.

Research is the bridge between the two.
