# Daily MLB-LAB Workflow

## Phase 1: Data Collection

Gather:

- Today's schedule
- Starting pitchers
- Confirmed lineups
- Weather
- Betting markets

Output:

daily-slate.md

---

## Phase 2: Research

Analyze:

- Environment
- Pitcher vulnerabilities
- Pitch arsenals
- Batter matchups
- Recent form

Output:

research-report.md

---

## Phase 3: Intelligence

Apply scoring model.

Environment = 20

Pitcher Vulnerability = 25

Pitch Arsenal = 20

Batter Matchup = 20

Statcast Confirmation = 15

Output:

ranked-opportunities.md

---

## Phase 4: Decision Support

Generate:

- Top plays
- Watchlist
- Risk factors
- Research notes

Output:

daily-mlb-report.md
