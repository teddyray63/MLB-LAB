# MLB-LAB Principles

## Core Philosophy

Research before modeling.

Understand the data before making predictions.

Intelligence is more valuable than information.

The goal is not to collect data.

The goal is to make better decisions.

---

## Evaluation Framework

Every repository must answer:

1. What does it do?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What can be reused?
4. What assumptions does it make?
5. What could break?
6. What should be ignored?

---

## Success Criteria

A repository is valuable if it improves:

- Data quality
- Research quality
- Decision quality
- Reporting quality

Not simply because it contains a model.

---

## MLB-LAB Mission

Convert baseball data into actionable intelligence.
